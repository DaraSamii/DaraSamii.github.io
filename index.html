About Me

I'm a Master's candidate in Mechanical Engineering with a background in Chemical Engineering, focused on integrating machine learning with computational fluid dynamics.

My research explores how Graph Neural Networks can accelerate CFD simulations, achieving 100× speedup over traditional solvers. I execute RANS, DDES, and aeroacoustic simulations on HPC clusters (400+ CPU cores, Compute Canada) for thermal spray coating analysis.

I lead CFD simulations for SpaceConcordia's solid rocketry team (CR25 and CR26 rockets) and hold an L2 rocketry certification from the Tripoli Rocketry Association.
